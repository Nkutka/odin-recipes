Pesto Pasta

A green delicious pasta which chicken

Ingredients

  • 250g pesto pasta
  • 650g chicken breast diced
  • 1 red capsicum, 1 green capsicum
  • Olives
  • Sundried potatoes
  • Fetta
  • 365g pasta

Method

  1. Cook pasta as per packet directions
  2. Cook chicken until browned. Remove
  3. Cut capsicum unto strips and cook
  4. Add pesto sauce, olives, sundried tomatoes and fetta, cook for 1 minutes
  5. Add chicken and cook until chicken is cooked through
  6. Sever pasta with chicken sauce on top.
