Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic tomato pasta meal which is easy to make and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 4 Rashers of bacon
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 spris of rosemary
  • 500g beef mince
  • 800g diced tomatoes
  • Basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 6 cherry tomatoes
  • 75g parmesean
  • 400g spaghetti

Method

  1. Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp.
  3. Chop finely, reduce heat and add onions, carrots, celery, garlic and rosemary, fry for 10 mins.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook stirring for 3-4 mins until the meat is browned all over.
  5. Add diced tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from the basil, oregano, bay leaves, beef stock cube and 6 halved cherry tomatoes.
  6. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 1 hr 15 mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.
  7. Add the 75g grated parmesan, check the seasoning and stir.
  8. When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  9. Drain the spaghetti and serve the sauce on top.
