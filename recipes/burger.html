Beef and Bacon Burger

Everyone loves burgers!

Ingredients

  • Mince beef
  • Cheese
  • Cos lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced pickles
  • Mayonaise
  • Siracha sauce
  • Bacon
  • Brioche buns

Method

  1. Form mince into flat patties
  2. Dice bacon
  3. Cook Bacon until golden and crispy
  4. Cook beef patties 4 minutes per side
  5. When beef is almost done add slice of chees on top to melt it
  6. Toast buns until golden
  7. Assemble burger with all toppings
